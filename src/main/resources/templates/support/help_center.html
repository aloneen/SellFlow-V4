Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on 'Register' in the top navigation bar and fill out the registration form with your username, email, phone number, and password.
How can I post a new product?
You must be logged in. Click on 'Sell' in the navigation menu, fill out the product form, upload images, and submit!
What should I do if I forget my password?
Currently, password recovery is not available. Please create a new account if you lose access to your old one.
How do I edit or delete my product listing?
Go to your 'Profile' page, find the product, and click 'Edit' or 'Delete' next to the product you want to modify.

Troubleshooting

  • If you can't upload images, make sure each file is less than 2MB and in JPG, PNG format.
  • If you cannot log in, ensure your username and password are entered correctly.
  • Clear your browser cache if you encounter display issues.

Getting Started Tutorials

  1. Create an account by registering with your details.
  2. Log in to your new account from the Sign In page.
  3. Navigate to 'Sell' to post your first product.
  4. Manage your listings from your 'Profile' page.
