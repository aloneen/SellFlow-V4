Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how SellFlow collects, uses, protects, and handles your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us when you register, create a profile, list a product, or contact support. This includes your username, email address, phone number, and any other information you voluntarily submit.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your products on the platform
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Send you important updates regarding your account

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and gather usage analytics. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including encryption, access control, and secure server hosting. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except when required by law or necessary to provide our services (e.g., payment processing).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
